Magic and whimsy are all around us if you can take the time to observe, imagine, and dream. I aim to bring magic to life's mundane and normal everyday things. I am inspired by the beauty of the sky day and night, sunsets and sunrises, and nature. People ask me why I see things the way I do. My response is well, why can't I?

From an early age, you learn about all this magic and have this wonder for the world around you. Why stop when you become an adult? My goal for the work is to bring a sense of magic, amusement, and wonder to the people around me so they remember to dream.
